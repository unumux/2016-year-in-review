projects

streamlining online support channels (faqs)

To optimize segmentation and create greater engagement, frequently asked questions (from our contact center) were created on coloniallife.com.

career seekers

To enable stronger recruiting, User Experience streamlined the career seekers experience on coloniallife.com with advanced architecture, design, and content.

Segmentations were adjusted to include home office and sales positions. The sales positions also have advance segmentation to help focus on Military Veterans, Hispanic Bilingual Professionals, and Students / Grads.

youville enhancements

account creation

Youville was upgraded to allow Colonial Life staff to create their own accounts. Before the enhancements, this process required submission of a form to request an account be developed manually. As of December 2016, 635 total sites have been created for accounts this year.

spanish support

We also created a parallel version of Youville in Spanish to support audiences that are more comfortable with Spanish than English.

youville stats

In 2016, Youville sites have received more than 100,000 visits from 70,000 users. The quiz has been completed 43,000 times.

dental product pdf converter

Supporting the launch of Colonial Life dental, a converter was created to enable state variations to be produced online in an easy and adaptable manner.

This enables pdfs to be produced with this variable data in just a few minutes.

broker extranet enhancements

Enhancements to the architecture, navigation, content and layout have been made to the my.coloniallife.com/brokers site to allow for a more comprehensive experience that aligns closer to the way brokers do business.

visual refresh

Colonial Life's flagship public site underwent a visual refresh to update imagery and iconography and improve accessibility.
