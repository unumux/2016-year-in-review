product comparison tool

This tool allows sales to be educated and prepared on competitive products in order to answer questions from prospects.

Our Competitive Intelligence partners spent 8 - 10 hours per week running comparisons with a 3 - 5 day turnaround. Now sales can independently pull the information at will without relying on Competitive Intelligence to pull reports for them. This resulted in a lift of 6,237 comparisons in 2016 vs 2,317 comparisons in 2015.

*Number of comparisons: 193 monthly in 2015 for a total of 2,317 vs 567 monthly in 2016 for a total of 6,237 total comparisons (February 1, 2016 - December 7, 2016).
