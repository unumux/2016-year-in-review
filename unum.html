field office pages

Discovery sessions within the field revealed the need for a better online representation in market to be more competitive and direct with sales opportunities.

A pilot version of new field office pages was tested with 5 offices to segment users for services needs, create greater engagement online, and drive direct contact to sales.

Live sites will be available to the full field by December 31, 2016.
