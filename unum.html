field office pages (pilot)

Discovery sessions within the field revealed the need for a better online representation in market to be more competitive and direct with sales opportunities.

A pilot version of the new field office pages was tested with 5 offices to segment users for services needs, create greater engagement online, and drive direct contact to sales.

*Will be available to the full field by 12/31/2016

benefits of one (integrated value proposition)

A value proposition site was built to showcase the benefits and simplicity of doing business with Unum.

A customized solution was built into the site to personalize sales connections and contacts for the field.

leadership development programs
